## 1. Why the strange names? (The "Vibe" naming)

| Modern Name            | What it actually is                      | Why we call it that                                                                |

## 2. Is it necessary to learn them?

**Yes and No.**

- **NO for Coding**: You don't need the name to write the CSS. `display: grid` is still just `display: grid`.
- **YES for Business**: If a high-paying client says, _"I want a Apple-style Bento layout for my dashboard,"_ and you know exactly what they mean, you instantly look like a **Senior Expert**. It builds trust.

---

## 3. How to "Learn" this (The 80/20 Rule)

You don't need a textbook. You only need two steps:

1.  **Vibe Check**: Look at [Mobbin](https://mobbin.com) or [Lapa Ninja](https://lapa.ninja) once a week for 5 minutes. You'll start seeing "Bento Grids" everywhere.
2.  **Component Library Scanning**: When you use a library like **Magic UI** or **Aceternity**, just look at the titles.
    - If you see a cool effect, just remember: _"Oh, that's called a Beam/Border/Glow."_
